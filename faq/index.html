FAQ

How do you gather presence information?
We listen to wifi probes sent by phones, tablets and computers. By listing and calculating their duration and strength through our algorithms we are able to offer clear concise information on peoples activity in the vicinity of your venue.
Do we give away nominal information about wifi users?
No we don’t. We aggregate the users information and implement it into your analytics but you cannot check what a specific person did while connected to your wifi.
